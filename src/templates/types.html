Types of Aluminum Wires

Aluminum Alloy Variants

  • AA-1350: High-purity aluminum used for electrical conductors, offering excellent conductivity.
  • AA-8000 Series: Advanced alloys designed for building wiring, improving safety and creep resistance.

Insulated vs. Bare Wires

  • PVC-Insulated Wires: Used in residential and commercial wiring for safety and insulation.
  • Bare Aluminum Conductors: Common in overhead power lines due to their lightweight nature.

Specialized Wires

  • Stranded Wires: Offer flexibility for applications requiring bending, such as automotive wiring.
  • Coated Wires: Enhanced with coatings for corrosion resistance or improved performance in extreme conditions.
